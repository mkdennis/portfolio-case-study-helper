## What milestone did you reach?
Launched smart category picker to 10% of sellers as an A/B test.

## What were the results?
- Upload completion rate increased by 23% compared to control
- Average time to complete upload decreased from 4.2 min to 2.8 min
- Category accuracy (user kept the suggestion) was 78%
- NPS for upload flow increased from 32 to 51

## What's next?
Planning full rollout next week. Also exploring extending the same pattern to auto-fill other fields like brand, condition, and pricing suggestions.
